साहित्यShala

कबीर         राम रस           निर्माता  




राम रस मीठो घणो
- कबीर



Bhajan Explanation


माला जपूँ ना कर जपूँ,
मुख से कहूँ ना राम,
राम हमारा हमें जपे,
हम पायो बिसराम...

In this couplet, saint Kabir said that he doesn't need to chaint Ram’s name or need any rosary beads
to turn as after finding his true self,
Kabir is at ease and lord Ram himself chaints Kabir’s name(in appreciation). 


कबीरा मन निर्मल भया, 
जैसे गंगा नीर,
पाछे लागा हरी फिरे,
 कहत "कबीर, कबीर…

Kabir here implies that as now his soul is as pure as holy river “ganga”,
God is following me and calling my name, instead of the opposite.


In both of the above couplets, Kabir is trying to teach us that
only chanting God’s name everyday Won’t help you find peace after life if your mind,
body and soul is polluted and if you’re not following God’s teachings.



 
मैं मेरा घर जाळिया रे, जोगिया जी,
लियो पलीता हाथ,
कोई अगर जाळो घर आपरो रे, जोगिया जी,
चलो हमारे साथ...

Standing in middle of the city with a fire torch(पलीता) in his hand,
Kabir is telling everyone that he has already burnt down his house,
As in, he’s leaving this perishable life of temporary attachments and desires.

He’s asking from every other yogi (जोगिया जी) , if they also are willing
to leave this temporary life They can come along with Kabir on his journey. 


 
हे राम रस मीठो घणो रे, जोगिया जी,
पिए अमर होई जाए...

Kabir here is telling them about his journey to attain RAM RAS (the drink of lord Ram)
Which is as sweet as nectar,and whoever gets it becomes immortal
as he will attain peace and will be remembered even after his death.

Here “RAM RAS” also refers to the teachings of lord Rama.
Actually, Kabir is convincing others to follow the footprint of lord Ram,
who left his kingdom and comfort and in the end defeated the evil to find success and peace.



 
घर जाळिया घर ऊभरे रे, जोगिया जी,
घर राख्यो घर जाए,
एक अचंभो मैं देखियो रे, जोगिया जी,
मड़ौ काल ने खाये,
हे राम रस…

Kabir is telling them that if you’ll burn down your temporary house,
A new house will grow inside Your soul where God will reside.
But if you’ll stick to this house (temporary life) it eventually
will be concluded (mortal life will be over after your death).

He told them that he saw a very strange(अचंभो) scenario
where a dead man(मड़ौ) is eating up time.
He meant that in this cycle of life, we all are eating up time
by wasting it on temporary desires, and in the end we’ll actually be dead,leaving all behind. 



 
 
आगे रे आगे दव जले रे, जोगिया जी,
पीछे हरिया है,
बलिहारी इण रूंखड़ी रे, जोगिया जी,
जड़ काट्यो फल होए,
हे राम रस…

Kabir says “o yogi !” I know the path ahead, as we go on and on, is as difficult as blaze and fire (दव जले),
And what we’re leaving behind is greenery(हरिया) , as in ,
Easy path of Comfort and desires. But if you’ll sacrifice(बलिहारी) your temporary life
In this soil(रूंखड़ी) or journey to find your inner self.
Then even if your roots will be cut down(जड़ काट्यो) ,you’ll receive the fruit (फल होए).

Kabir here meant that, even when you’ll be dead and will leave this body you’ll attain moksha And peace. 



 
ध्रुव पियो, प्रहलाद पियो रे, जोगिया जी,
पियो पीपे रविदास,
भगत कबीरो पिए रह्यो रे, जोगिया जी,
फेर पीयण री आस,
हे राम रस…

Kabir told them about some famous personalities like “Dhruv”(Pole star),
“Prahlad”(From Satyug) and “Peepa Ravidas”(student teacher duo) and
how they also drank “RAM RAS” and attain inner peace by following
the teachings of God and leaving this temporary world behind.

In the end, He also told them about how he’s also drinking the RAM RAS and
Hopes to do the same again in his next coming births.


राम नाम की लूट है, लूट सके तो लूट।
अंत समय पछतायेगा, जब प्राण जायेंगे छूट॥
